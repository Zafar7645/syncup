# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

SyncUp is a learning-first open source project. We are committed to making participation a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, background, experience level, nationality, or identity.

## Our Standards

**Examples of behaviour that contributes to a positive environment:**

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members

**Behaviour that is not acceptable includes:**

- Personal attacks or insults
- Public or private harassment of any kind
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Any conduct that a reasonable person would consider inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behaviour may be reported by contacting the project maintainer at **zafar.shaikh.se@gmail.com**. All reports will be reviewed promptly and handled confidentially.

The maintainer has the right to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct, and to temporarily or permanently ban any contributor for behaviour deemed inappropriate.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces — GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other forum where the project is represented.
